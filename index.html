About GHA

Rwanda's Most Celebrated School

Founded in 1997, Green Hills Academy has grown from 130 learners to one of Africa's most internationally respected schools. We are Rwanda's only full-continuum IB World School — offering an unbroken educational journey from Nursery through Grade 12.

Our 26-acre campus in Nyarutarama hosts over 2,200 learners from 60+ nationalities, taught by a world-class multinational faculty committed to academic excellence and the whole development of every child.
