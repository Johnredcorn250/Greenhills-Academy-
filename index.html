IB Curriculum

Four Programmes,
One Seamless Journey

Rwanda's only full-continuum IB school, offering an unbroken educational pathway from Nursery 1 through Grade 12 — all on one connected campus.

🌿
PYP
Primary Years Programme
Nursery 1 – Grade 5

Inquiry-based, student-centred learning that cultivates curiosity, agency, and a love of discovery. Available in English-only and bilingual French/English tracks.

🔭
MYP
Middle Years Programme
Grades 6 – 10

A holistic framework connecting academic subjects to real-world contexts. Builds critical thinking, self-management, and intercultural awareness across eight subject groups.

🎓
DP
Diploma Programme
Grades 11 – 12

The gold standard in pre-university education. GHA graduates consistently earn admission to competitive universities worldwide including Oxford, MIT, and leading African institutions.

🛠️
CP
Career-related Programme
Grades 11 – 12

A rigorous blend of academic study, real-world professional skills, and personal development — preparing learners for higher education, professional training, or entrepreneurship.

Boarding

A Home
Away from Home

Our boarding programme gives learners from across Rwanda and around the world the chance to fully immerse themselves in GHA life — academically, socially, and personally.

🛏️
Comfortable, Supervised Accommodation Separate boarding houses for boys and girls in Grades 7–12, with full-time houseparents and pastoral support.
🍽️
Nutritious Daily Meals Our on-campus dining hall serves three balanced meals daily, with a focus on fresh, locally-sourced Rwandan produce.
📚
Structured Study & Recreation Supervised evening study sessions, weekend activities, and access to all campus facilities including pools, gym, and library.

How to Apply

Your Journey
Starts Here

1
Submit an Online Application Complete our OpenApply application form for your child's target year group. Applications are accepted year-round.
2
Assessment & Interview Our admissions team will review your application and invite you and your child for a school visit and assessment.
3
Offer & Enrolment Upon a successful assessment, you'll receive a formal offer. Confirm your place with the enrolment deposit to secure your child's spot.
4
Welcome to GHA Join our orientation programme, meet your child's teachers and class, and begin an extraordinary educational journey.
